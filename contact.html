Future Work

In the future, it is recommended to continue adding data from each coming year in order to get a current and precise answer to all questions. By gathering more data about which foods are more susceptible to contamination, changes could be made in the food industry to improve the quality and cleanliness. Also, further research about new species and mutation in the microbiology field could prevent outbreaks and help cure victims. This will ultimately lead to less illnesses, less hospitalizations, and less deaths from foodborne illnesses.
